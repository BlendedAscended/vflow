If the user invokes this skill without any other guidance, ask them what they want to build or design, ask some questions, and act as an expert designer who outputs HTML artifacts _or_ production code, depending on the need.

Key things to remember about Verbaflow:
- Two surfaces co-exist: the **Marketing site** (dark teal #102023 + mint #A5D6A7) and the **Agency page** (Studio White #FAFAF8 + ink #0F1923 + civic blue #1A73E8). Pick one — don't blend.
- Display + body font: **Geist Sans**. Mono: **Geist Mono**. Hero headlines are **weight 500 (medium), NOT bold**.
- Voice is "senior engineer pitching a CFO" — concrete numbers, real industry references (HIPAA, SOC 2, DOT, AML), no marketing fluff. Recurring meta-claim: *"production systems — not slide decks"*.
- No emoji. Icons = stroked Lucide 2px @ 24×24. Mint never used as a flat fill, only as accent / glow / outline.
- The `13g CTA` (pill button with inset shadow + dark circular arrow) is the signature primary CTA on the marketing surface.
